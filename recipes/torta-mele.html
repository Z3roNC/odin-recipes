Italian Apple Cake



This Italian apple cake recipe is from my mom and reminds me of my childhood.

It tastes delicious, and I bake it for my family now.


First look about:

  • Prep: 20 mins
  • Cook: 30 mins
  • Total: 50 mins
  • Servings: 8
  • Yield: 8 servings

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons unseasoned bread crumbs, or as needed
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, melted
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • 1 vanilla bean, split and scraped
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 ⅛ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 tablespoon milk, or as needed (Optional)
  • 2 pounds Granny Smith apples, peeled, cored and sliced thin
  • ¼ cup unsalted butter, cubed
  • ¼ cup white sugar

Directions:

  1. Step 1

    reheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).

    Grease a pie plate and dust with bread crumbs

  2. Step 2

    Combine melted butter, 1/2 cup sugar, and vanilla bean seeds in a bowl.

    Add eggs and stir to combine. Mix flour, baking powder,

    nd salt in a bowl and add to butter mixture.

    Add milk if batter is too thick

  3. Step 3

    Pour batter into the prepared pie plate.

    Distribute apple slices in a thick layer on top of the batter.

    Dot with cubed butter and sprinkle with 1/4 cup sugar.

  4. Step 4

    Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted

    into the center comes out clean, 30 to 45 minutes.

    Cool on a wire rack for 5 minutes.

    Run a table knife around the edges to loosen.

    nvert carefully onto a serving plate or cooling rack. Let cool completely.

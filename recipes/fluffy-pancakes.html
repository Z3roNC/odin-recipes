Fluffy Japanese Pancakes



Fluffy Japanese pancakes.

These impressively tall pancakes are great with maple syrup!

You will need enough 3 1/2-inch ring molds to fill

your frying pan for this recipe.


First look about:

  • Prep: 10mins
  • Cook: 15mins
  • Total: 25mins
  • Servings: 8
  • Yield: 8 pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs, separated
  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • ¼ cup white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 ¼ cups buttermilk
  • ¼ cup melted butter
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions:

  1. Step 1

    Beat egg whites in a glass, metal, or ceramic bowl until stiff peaks form.

  2. Step 2

    Butter enough 3 1/2-inch ring molds to fill your frying pan.

    Place molds in the pan over low heat. Cover.

  3. Step 3

    Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and baking soda in a bowl.

    Add the flour mixture and stir until batter is fairly smooth.

  4. Step 4

    Combine buttermilk, egg yolks, butter, and vanilla extract in a separate bowl.

  5. Step 5

    Fold egg whites into the batter until combined.

    Small bits of egg whites still showing is ok.

  6. Step 6

    Pour about 1/2 cup of batter into each mold and cover the pan.

    Cook until bubbles start forming at the top, about 5 minutes.

    Flip pancakes in their molds and cook until set, 3 to 4 minutes more.

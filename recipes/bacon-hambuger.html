Cheddar Bacon Hamburgers



A great and easy way to add some flavor to your hamburgers!


First look about:

  • Prep: 5 mins
  • Cook: 15 mins
  • Total: 20 mins
  • Servings: 4
  • Yield: 4 burgers

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • ½ cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 2 tablespoons prepared horseradish
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon pepper
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ½ cup real bacon bits
  • 4 hamburger buns

Directions:

  1. Step 1

    Preheat grill for high heat.

  2. Step 2

    In a large bowl, mix together the ground beef, Cheddar cheese,

    horseradish, salt, pepper, garlic powder, and bacon bits using your hands.

    Shape the mixture into 4 hamburger patties.

  3. Step 3

    Lightly oil the grill grate.

    Place hamburger patties on the grill, and cook for 5 minutes

    per side, or until well done. Serve on buns.
